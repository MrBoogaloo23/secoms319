NBA 2021-2022 Season

Welcome! Our website provides comprehensive coverage of the 2021-2022 NBA season, including team and player standings

The NBA season is an annual professional basketball league featuring 30 teams that play 82 games each between October and June, with playoffs taking place between April and June. The season is known for its high level of competition and some of the world's best basketball players compete for the championship title. The season includes events such as the All-Star Game and the NBA Draft, and offers exciting moments and close finishes for fans watching online, on TV, or in-person. On this website you can find out more about what happened in the 2021-2022 NBA season and look at the stats from that season.
